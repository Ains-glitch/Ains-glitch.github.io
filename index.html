Identifying the Solar Sources of Ambient Solar Wind-Driven Substorms with the ADAPT-WSA Model

Investigating geoeffective solar wind formation linked to substorm activity using the ADAPT-WSA model. Developing methods to classify solar wind properties and identify source regions associated with geomagnetic substorms, excluding large-scale solar events.
